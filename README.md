# PSPO I Exam Simulation

## Overview

This project contains an interactive **Professional Scrum Product Owner (PSPO I)** exam simulation tool. It's designed to help candidates prepare for the official PSPO I certification exam by providing a realistic practice environment.

## Contents

- **pspo1_exam_simulation.html** - Interactive HTML-based exam simulator with:
  - PSPO I exam questions
  - Timed test simulation
  - Instant feedback on answers
  - Progress tracking
  - Score calculation

## Features

- 🎯 **Realistic Exam Simulation** - Practice questions that mirror the actual PSPO I exam format
- ⏱️ **Timed Mode** - Test yourself under exam time constraints
- 📊 **Score Tracking** - Track your performance and progress
- ✅ **Instant Feedback** - Get immediate feedback on your answers
- 🎨 **Modern UI** - Clean, professional interface with smooth interactions

## How to Use

1. **Open the File**: Open `pspo1_exam_simulation.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Start the Exam**: Click the "Start Exam" button to begin the simulation
3. **Answer Questions**: Read each question carefully and select your answer
4. **Submit Answers**: Review your answers and submit when ready
5. **View Results**: Check your score and review explanations for each question

## Browser Compatibility

## Study Tips

- Take this exam multiple times to improve your score
- Review the questions you got wrong and understand the concepts
- Time yourself to practice completing the exam within the time limit
- Use this as a supplement to official Scrum.org study materials

## About PSPO I Certification

The **Professional Scrum Product Owner (PSPO I)** certification validates your knowledge of:
- Product Owner responsibilities
- Scrum framework fundamentals
- Product management best practices
- Agile principles and values
- Stakeholder management

For more information, visit [Scrum.org](https://www.scrum.org/)

## Notes

- This is a practice/study tool - scores here may not reflect actual exam performance
- Always refer to official Scrum.org resources for the most current exam information
